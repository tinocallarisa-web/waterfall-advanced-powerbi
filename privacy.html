Privacy Policy

Product: Waterfall Advanced  |  Publisher: TCViz  |  Last updated: 2025-05-01

1. Data Collection

Waterfall Advanced does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data outside your Power BI environment. All data processing occurs locally within your Power BI report session.

2. Data Processed Locally

The visual processes only the data you explicitly add to its data roles (Category, Value, Target, Bar Type, Sort Order). This data is used exclusively to render the waterfall chart within your report and is never sent to any external server by TCViz.

3. AI Narratives (Finance Tier)

The Finance tier includes an optional AI narrative feature. When you use this feature, the aggregated chart data (totals, variances, driver names and values) is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you select (Anthropic or OpenAI) using your own API key. TCViz does not receive, store, or have access to this data or your API key at any point.

Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI provider:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

4. License Verification

License status is verified through Microsoft's official IVisualLicenseManager API, which communicates with Microsoft AppSource infrastructure. TCViz does not receive or store any personal information through this process.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This visual does not use cookies, local storage, or any tracking technologies.

6. Third-Party Services

Other than the optional AI provider connection described in Section 3, this visual does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

7. Children's Privacy

This visual is a business intelligence tool not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13 years of age.
