## Long description (3000 chars max)

**Waterfall Advanced** is a professional-grade waterfall chart for Power BI, designed for finance, sales, and commercial excellence teams who need more than the built-in waterfall visual.

### Key features

**Full waterfall control**
Connect any category column and value measure to build a waterfall. Define bar types (total, subtotal, delta) via a dedicated column, or let the visual auto-detect them. Sort deltas by impact with one click.

**Subtotals and targets**
Add intermediate subtotal bars at any position. Connect a target measure to display target lines on total bars, with gap indicators.

**Anomaly detection**
Bars that deviate more than 2 standard deviations from the mean are automatically flagged with a visual indicator — no configuration needed.

**Summary cards**
Five KPI cards below the chart: initial total, final total, variance %, biggest gain, and biggest loss. Toggle on/off.

**Rich formatting**
Full color control for positive, negative, total, and target bars. Label modes: absolute, relative %, or both. Configurable number format with currency symbol (€, $, £, ¥), scale (auto/k/M), and decimals.

**Cross-filtering**
Click any bar to filter other visuals on the page. Selected bars are highlighted; others dim automatically.

**Multilingual**
Interface available in English, Spanish, French, and German.
